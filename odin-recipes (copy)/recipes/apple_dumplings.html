Apple Dumplings

Return home

A sweet treat enjoyed by many throughout Skyrim.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet puff pastry sheets
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 1/ cup brown sugar
  • 6 peeled and diced apples
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Roll out pastry and cut into 5 inch squares.
  3. Grease a baking tray and set aside.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine diced apples, cinnamon, nutmeg, vanilla extract, and half the butter. Mix well.
  5. Pour the apple mixture onto the center of each pastry square and fold the four corners of each pastry toward the center. Pinch the center with wet fingers to keep the pastry together.
  6. Separate dumplings on the baking tray so they do not stick together.
  7. Brush dumplings with remaining melted butter.
  8. Bake for 30-45 minutes until golden brown, remove from oven, and enjoy.
