Potage le Magnifique

Return home

Signature dish of the most famous chef in Tamriel, The Gourmet.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup plain flour
  • 100g butter or olive oil
  • 5 cloves garlic chopped
  • 2 red onions chopped
  • 1 leek chopped
  • 3 carrots peeled and chopped
  • 1 potato peeled and diced
  • 1 tbsp dried herb seasoning blend of choice
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Instructions

  1. In a pot, melt the butter and fry the leeks on medium heat until softened. Add onions and garlic and fry until browned.
  2. Pour in white wine and cook for two minutes.
  3. Add potatoes, carrots, water, and broth.
  4. Sift in flour while stirring constantly to avoid clumping.
  5. Add herbs, bay leaf, and salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Simmer on low heat while covered for 30 minutes, or until potatoes soften.
  7. Remove bay leaf and pour soup into food processor or blender.
  8. Blend until smooth and serve hot.
