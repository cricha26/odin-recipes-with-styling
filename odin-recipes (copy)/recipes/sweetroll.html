"Let me guess... someone stole your Sweetroll?"

Ingredients

Rolls

  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 sticks butter
  • 5 eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Glaze

  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

Rolls

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Beat butter and sugar together until fluffy.
  3. Slowly add eggs and milk to the butter and sugar mixture while beating.
  4. Add the flour, salt, and vanilla extract and stir gently until mixed.
  5. Pour into greased pans and bake until golden, about 20 minutes.

Glaze

  1. Melt the butter and combine with milk in a bowl.
  2. Mix the powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Pour the mixture over the finished and cooled rolls.
